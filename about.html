About Us

20 Years of Focus, Professional Excellence

Company Overview

Zhuji Rongxing Knitting Co., Ltd. was established on May 28, 2002. We are a professional socks manufacturer with over 20 years of experience in R&D, quality control, and service.

Our factory covers an area of 27,800 square meters and is recognized as a scale enterprise by the local government. We have 800 professional sock machines with a monthly production capacity of 3 million pairs, mainly exported to European and American markets.

The company currently employs 160 staff, including 10 technical development engineers and 20 maintenance engineers. All sock production processes are completed in-house, with strict quality control management at every stage to ensure stable and reliable product quality.
