FAQ

Answers to common questions about cooperation

Q: What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

A: Our standard MOQ is 3,000 pairs/color/style. For long-term partners or large volume orders, we can negotiate more flexible MOQs.

Q: How long does sampling take?

A: Regular sampling takes 7-10 working days. Complex processes or special materials may require 10-15 working days. Sampling fees can be deducted upon bulk order placement.

Q: What is the lead time for bulk orders?

A: Depending on order quantity and process complexity, bulk order lead time is typically 30-45 days. We will provide an accurate delivery schedule upon order confirmation.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept T/T wire transfer, L/C letter of credit, and other payment methods. Specific payment terms are negotiated based on order amount and cooperation history.

Q: Do you provide packaging design services?

A: Yes, we can provide packaging design services including hangtags, labels, and packaging boxes. You may also provide your own design files.

Q: How do you ensure product quality?

A: We have a strict quality control process with 6 inspection stages from raw materials to finished products. All products meet international quality standards.
